Dear Representative Gillen,

I am a homeowner in Rockville Centre and one of your constituents. This is a courtesy note before a nonpartisan site I built goes public on August 1: rvc-taxes.jeffpinto.com. I wanted your office to see it first.

The site connects two facts. Our school district passed its 2026-27 budget at $141,323,369 on May 19, cutting 22.2 teaching positions and 40 teaching-assistant positions to stay under the tax cap. At the same time, 2,076 of our owner-occupied homes, about 27.9 percent, have a homeowner aged 65 or older, many in larger houses that would fit the younger families the schools need. Enrollment has fallen from 3,533 students in 2015-16 to 3,276, and the district projects 3,260 next year. Every figure on the site links to its primary public source on a facts page.

One reason seniors who want to move stay put is the federal home-sale capital gains exclusion, unchanged since 1997. H.R. 1340, the More Homes on the Market Act, doubles and indexes it and has 146 cosponsors from both parties, including Rep. Suozzi and Rep. Malliotakis. The site's federal ask is simple: please cosponsor H.R. 1340. Seniors who want to downsize should not face a five-figure tax bill for doing it.

One thing I would ask now: if it would help, I would welcome 15 minutes to walk you or your staff through the one-page deck before launch. Reply and I will send times that work for your office.

Thank you for your time.
