Neighbors,

I put together a nonpartisan site about something a lot of us have been talking about, and it is live today: rvc-taxes.jeffpinto.com.

Here is the short version. This spring our school district passed its budget after cutting 22.2 teaching positions and 40 teaching-assistant positions to stay under the tax cap. Enrollment has dropped from 3,533 students in 2015-16 to 3,276. At the same time, 2,076 of our owner-occupied homes, about 27.9 percent, have a homeowner aged 65 or older, and many are larger houses. When an older neighbor who wants to downsize can move without a tax penalty, one of those homes opens up for a younger family, which steadies enrollment and spreads the levy. Nobody is asking anyone to leave. A senior who wants to stay put should be left exactly as they are today.

The site lays out a few specific, low-cost fixes at the federal, state, and county level, and every number on it links to its primary public source so you can check it yourself.

One thing to do today: go to rvc-taxes.jeffpinto.com, type your address, and it will show you your actual representatives and give you a ready-to-send letter for each one. It takes about two minutes.

Please pass this along to anyone in town who might want it.

Thanks,
Jeff Pinto
Rockville Centre
