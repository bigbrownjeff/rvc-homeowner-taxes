The closure file — how current is "the endgame," really?

Northport–East Northport
(Suffolk) · Aug 2021

Two elementary schools actually closed — Dickinson Avenue and Bellerose Avenue (board vote 5–2, Apr 29, 2021). Stated drivers: enrollment down 10.6% in six years (5,748 → 5,138) and the LIPA settlement stepping payments from $86M to $46M; ~$7M/yr saved. Northport Journal · Patch

Three Village (Suffolk)
voted Jan 2024 · eff. fall 2025

District-wide grade consolidation — 6th into the junior highs, 9th into Ward Melville HS — after enrollment fell ~23% from 2012-13. Buildings stayed open; the structure didn't. TBR News Media

Long Beach (Nassau)
Jan 2025

Nassau's nearest miss — East Elementary studied for closure for a year; a 4,000-signature petition and packed meetings reversed it ("for the foreseeable future"). The fight, not the outcome, is the data point: closure talk has reached Nassau. Patch · News 12

North Shore CSD (Nassau)
2022–2028

The fiscal-cliff companion: the LIPA Glenwood Landing settlement strips >$8M by 2027-28; the homeowner share of the levy has already climbed from ~70% to over 80%. LI Press
