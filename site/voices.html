The closure file: how current is "the endgame," really?
Northport-East Northport
Aug 2021

Two elementary schools actually closed (board vote 5-2). Drivers: enrollment down 10.6% in six years and the LIPA settlement stepping payments from $86M to $46M; ~$7M/yr saved. Northport Journal

Three Village
voted Jan 2024

District-wide grade consolidation after enrollment fell ~23% from 2012-13. Buildings stayed open; the structure did not. TBR News Media

Long Beach
Jan 2025

Nassau's nearest miss. East Elementary was studied for closure for a year; a 4,000-signature petition and packed meetings reversed it. The fight, not the outcome, is the data point: closure talk has reached Nassau. Patch

North Shore CSD
2022-2028

The fiscal-cliff companion: the LIPA Glenwood Landing settlement strips over $8M by 2027-28; the homeowner share of the levy has climbed from ~70% to over 80%. LI Press
